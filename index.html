Product Description

The nature of our product comes down to connecting experts to clients who are in need of expert advice on their career. Our services provide one-on-one sessions where clients learn about the skills they need to gain, types of experiences required, places to volunteer, classes to take in college, and how to be job ready. Our clients will have a detailed action plan that will propel them to a successful career

Operations of Service

Connect with each other through video calls over the internet

Efficiency

Build your career profile upon registering with Xperia. Your career background and information is readily available for current and future coaches. Therefore, clients are not limited to a single coach, and more sessions are made available

Expertise

Our expert coaches have the background, knowledge, experience, and networks in the corresponding field of study

Personalization

Coaches are dedicated to individual clients over a period of time, and a client-mentor relationship is established

Market Analysis

  • • We are involved with the education and career industry which has always existed and will continue to be a successful area in the market
  • • The target market for this product are people who are in need of advice, direction, and a plan for their current or future career endeavors
  • • Our product and services are needed by students, graduates, and non-graduates all throughout the year

Market Strategy

  • • Growth Strategy
    •  Acquisitions are important to accelerate growth and scale. A horizontal strategy would be to provide the same product to customers ranging from high school and college students to working adults with or without college degrees.

  • • Channels of Distribution
    •  Our primary channel of distribution would be educational institutions such as high schools and colleges. We would connect, interact, and complete transactions with schools. The schools would then be responsible in handling and delivering our product and services to their students.

    Having schools as our partners will ensure the safety of having new customers, growth, and sustainability for the company each year.

    An internal sales force would send a team of representatives to connect with educational institutions across the country. Other channels of distribution include businesses and organizations that are tied closely with education such as book rental providers, tutor services, and job search services. A few to name are Wiley, Amazon, Chegg, WyzAnt, Indeed, and Hired.

  • • Communication Strategy
    •  The best part about having educational institutions as channels for distribution is that it makes the process of reaching students automated and much easier. Print, radio, public transportation, phone applications, television, and the internet are all mediums of advertising. However, this can get costly unless we narrow down our advertising. Therefore, acquiring or purchasing user data from data collecting services such as Google and Facebook would help narrow down our advertising, as well as cut costs
